Special Facilities

Canteen

A canteen is similar to a mess but offer additional food and beverage options beyond regular meals. It's often a convenient spot for Student to grab snacks, drinks, or light meals throughout the day, especially outside of regular meal times.

Library

Our hostel library provides a environment for learning and collaboration, offering 24/7 access, air-conditioned comfort, and central study tables. Each table is equipped with electricity for added convenience, fostering an ideal space for focused academic pursuits.

Sports Room

The sports room is where students can engage in various physical activities and sports. It is equipped with equipment for activities like Pool, table tennis, carrom, and other indoor games, providing students with opportunities for recreation and exercise.

Mess

The Mess exclusively serves Jain cuisine. It's a communal dining area where individuals gather to enjoy their meals together. Additionally, the Mess provides Tiffin services and accommodates guests for meals.

Gymnasium

The gym is a dedicated space for hosteller to engage in physical fitness activities and workouts. It's equipped with exercise machines, weights, and other equipment, allowing residents to maintain their health through regular exercise.

House Keeping

Housekeeping services are an integral part of our hostel it maintains a hygienic and comfortable environment . Our staff ensures that common areas such as corridors, rooms are regularly cleaned to uphold standards of cleanliness.
